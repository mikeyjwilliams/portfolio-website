Tools Site Project

Why Build?

So what was the reasoning behind this personal project?
The Reason:
My father retired from body and fender work and has decided he wants to sell his tools. Originally his plan was:

  • take pictures of each tool with phone
  • write out name, a item number, if more then one(most just one) write quantity and price.
  • fill out a description of each item next to item number and other info on paper.
  • write each one out, on front and back put phone number and name.
  • If someone was interested in something and wanted to see it he could send them a picture of the tool.
  • print multiple 10 page long lists out and take them to businesses that people might be interested work at.

The issues:

  • The lists are about 10 pages long...very thick.
  • Can not see item pictures without contacting owner first.
  • can only read description, can not see item with paper.
  • can not update list without printing a new one each time an item sells.
  • if there is a typo or anything needs to be changed must print all new lists out (10 pages each)

Solution

To build a data-driven website for the tools. Thinking of how to build out a solution, a normal website would not make sense. There would be lots of content that would be on multiple pages be duplicated, there would be hundreds of pages, and only certain cookie cutter parts would change. A database holding the information, seperate pages for header, footer, and a few special pages for the different types of middle content needed. That way if something was sold, price changed, or name changed. I could change it in the database for that specific item or type of item and not worry about the pages at all just adjust it in the database and all is well.

Thoughts

Being one of my first larger scale projects, this was an interesting one. It was very difficult due to being given the photos and not having any control of them. Just getting a list of paper with information and item numbers linking to pictures I had to go off of. It was very different, but educational and neat to build.
