About Michael

Greetings, my name is Michael Williams. I am an upcoming Software Engineer in Web Development. I enjoy puzzles, looking for solutions, and trying to make things better.

As of now I have a Bachelors in Software Engineering, and am enrolled in Lambda Schools Bootcamp for Web Development.
Also, I work in the grocery business as a cashier and have worked customer service in the past. I love to work with people and help them with technology and get the most out of what they are using and to help them learn the tricks and secrets of the tool or tech they are learning.

In my time deplugged from coding, I enjoy a few things around and away from the computer. Since I live in Florida I like to take advantage of that and go kayaking some and enjoy the outside weather when I can. I really enjoy music whether it is playing guitar, creating beats on the computer, trying to build songs from loops and beats, or just listening to other people's music. Another past time is art, I enjoy sketching, drawing renditions of popular cartoons, and I am currently trying to learn how to use a wacom tablet to draw digitally.
